// 02 — FLAGSHIP RESEARCH

HCASFE-Net

HCASFE-Net is a novel neural architecture built entirely from scratch in pure NumPy — no PyTorch, no TensorFlow — running on a phone. It combines five subsystems into a single unified forward pass.

Unlike flat models, HCASFE-Net builds explicit representations of time, causality, and memory. It detects when the world changes. It remembers what happened 100 steps ago. It learns which signals cause which outcomes.

Current results show the architecture winning against a flat MLP baseline on K=1 and K=5 lookahead prediction, 80% surprise detection at 76× the sensitivity of a trivial detector, and near-perfect causal regime fingerprinting at 96.5% accuracy.
